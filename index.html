How To Use
1. Enter a target
Type any IPv4 or IPv6 address (e.g. 1.1.1.1) into the input field.
2. Or check your own IP
Click "Analyze My IP" to automatically detect and scan your current public IP address.
3. Click Analyze
The scan runs server-side and returns location, network, and threat intelligence data.
4. Review the results
Check the map for location, the cards for ISP/network details, and the threat bar for VPN/Tor/Proxy flags.
5. Export if needed
Use "Copy Report" for a text summary or "Export JSON" to download the full raw data.
Questions & Answers
What does this tool do?
It looks up public intelligence on any IPv4 or IPv6 address — location, ISP, network info, and threat flags like VPN/Tor/Proxy detection.
Do I need an account?
No. This tool is completely free and requires no login or signup.
How do I check my own IP?
Click "Analyze My IP" — it automatically detects and scans your current public IP address.
Is my data stored?
No personal data is stored. Lookups are processed server-side and not saved.
What does VPN/Tor/Proxy detection mean?
These flags indicate whether the IP is known to belong to a VPN provider, Tor exit node, or proxy service — useful for spotting masked connections.
Are there more tools like this?
